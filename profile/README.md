## 🚀 Overview
ShareX for Windows is a free, all-in-one capture tool that replaces a handful of separate apps at once. Use it as a fast snipping tool to grab a region, window, or full screen, capture a whole scrolling page as one image, or switch to screen recording for video and GIF clips. Every capture can be marked up in the built-in editor, then saved locally or copied to your clipboard. With a single ShareX download, you get flexible hotkeys, automated workflows, and a stack of extra utilities that make capturing and sharing your screen effortless.

- **Flexible Screenshots** — Capture a region, active window, or the whole screen with your own hotkeys.
- **Screen Recorder** — Record smooth video or lightweight GIFs for demos, tutorials, and reports.
- **Scrolling Capture** — Turn a long page or document into a single, continuous screenshot.
- **Image Editor** — Add arrows, text, highlights, and blur to your captures before saving.
- **Custom Workflows** — Automate capture-to-save steps so your routine happens with one press.

## ❓ FAQ
**What is ShareX?**
ShareX is a free, open-source screenshot and screen recording tool for Windows. It brings screenshots, video recording, scrolling capture, an editor, and workflow automation together in one lightweight app.

**Is ShareX free?**
Yes. ShareX is completely free and open source, with every capture, recording, and editing feature included at no cost and no subscription.

**Which versions of Windows are supported?**
ShareX works on modern 64-bit versions of Windows, including Windows 10 and Windows 11. Keeping your system updated helps captures and recordings stay smooth.

**Is my data secure?**
Your screenshots and recordings are stored locally on your own computer, and nothing is uploaded unless you choose to share it. You stay in full control of your captures.
